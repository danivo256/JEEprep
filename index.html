How to Start Preparing for JEE from Scratch

Preparing for IIT-JEE can seem overwhelming, but with the right strategy, anyone can crack it. Begin by understanding the syllabus and identifying your strengths and weaknesses. Make a timetable that suits your learning style.

Focus on conceptual clarity instead of rote learning. Use NCERT books as your base and refer to standard books like H.C. Verma, I.E. Irodov, and N.Avasthi. Don't forget regular revision and mock tests.

Top 5 Mistakes JEE Aspirants Should Avoid

  • Ignoring NCERT basics
  • Not following a fixed schedule
  • Skipping mock tests
  • Over-reliance on coaching
  • Not maintaining health and sleep
